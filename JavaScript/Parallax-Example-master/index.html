Parallax Scrolling

Background moves slower

This first section of the site has the parallax effect by allowing the background to move at a slightly slower speed than the actual page content.

Two div backgrounds

There are two div tags in this section that have background images applied. They are set to move at differing speeds so that the foreground moves at the right speed but the backgrounds move at slower speeds.
